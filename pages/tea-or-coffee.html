Tea or Coffee

 

Imagine waking up to a cup full of warmth, love, peace and tranquility. Every sip draws your closer to nature and every different flavor takes you on a journey to a different country, the world in the palm of your hands. A simple tea bag can nurture both the body and the soul that’s why I choose Tea over Coffee.

Many might argue that Coffee has the ability to revitalize energy however I believe that I have a natural zest and hunger for life therefore I am always enthusiastic about taking on new challenges at any time of the day. Besides what is coffee without caffine? Studies have shown that most consumers drink coffee for the caffine which can be replaced by energy drinks, hence why Decaf coffee is not a favorite to many. Caffine is also known to be very addictive, so how do we really know that Coffee lovers really appreciate a cup of coffee?

A Tea bag can never be replaced however it can be amplified, Weather Served hot on a freezing day or cold on a Humid day (Ice Tea), we can all agree that after a cup of tea (Hot or Cold) we feel tranquil, composed and serene. It’s a medical fact that tea has the ability to lower one’s blood pressure which results in a reduction in risk of one dying from a stroke or heart attack. Green tea is a rich source of flavonoids, a bioactive compound that can lessen oxidative stress and relieve inflammation which I tend to suffer from.
